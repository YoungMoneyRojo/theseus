Theseus

Indoor navigation made simple

Interior Navigation Challenge

Background

  • Navigating large indoor complex spaces is often challenging due to complex layouts and the limitations of traditional aids like static maps, signs, and phone-based GPS.

What is Theseus?

  • Theseus is a python-based web application that helps users find their way through buildings through the usage of landmarks. Users can upload a photo of a landmark and recieve relative directions to their desired destination.
